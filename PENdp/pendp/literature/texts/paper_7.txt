NEWS SUMMARY (Yicai/First Finance, May 13, 2026)
================================================================================

According to Xinhua News Agency, the CDE published the "2025 Annual Drug Evaluation
Report" on May 13, 2026. Key highlights:

- 2025年药品注册申请受理、审结数量稳步增长
  (Drug registration applications received and reviewed showed steady growth in 2025)

- 创新药获批数量创历史新高
  (Number of innovative drug approvals reached a record high)

- 临床急需用药加速上市
  (Clinically urgent drugs accelerated to market)

- 药品审评审批体系持续完善
  (Drug evaluation and approval system continues to improve)

- 有力保障群众用药需求
  (Effectively guaranteeing public medication needs)

STRATEGIC RELEVANCE TO PENdp
================================================================================

1. 核酸药物LNP递送: Review channel not yet saturated — regulatory opportunity
   for LNP-based nucleic acid drugs
2. 多肽药物: Listed as one of four major innovation directions — supportive for
   PENdp's peptide-based LNP targeting approach
3. 附条件批准: Conditional approval pathway is mature — could accelerate
   clinical translation
4. 市场竞争: Few LNPs approved in China despite global advances (Onpattro,
   Comirnaty) — PENdp could be first-mover
